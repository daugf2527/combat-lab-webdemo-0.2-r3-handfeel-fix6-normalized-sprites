# DFO Official Berserker Balance Patch History

> Source: https://www.dfoneople.com/news/updates/1393/Character-Balance/Berserker
> Update: Operation: Hope (2019-06-18 UTC)
> Scraped: 2026-05-04

## Berserker Balance Changes

### Mountainous Wheel
- Downward strike Atk. decreased by **51.5%**
- Shock wave Atk. increased by **52.9%**

### Bloodlust
- Absorbing Atk. increased by **9%**
- Releasing Atk. increased by **12.2%**
- Additional Atk. against Bleeding enemies increased by **12.2%**
- Now concentrates and discharges blood if you grab a Grab-immune enemy (fallback behavior)

### Raging Fury
- Shock wave Atk. increased by **12.7%**
- Blood pillar Atk. decreased by **7.2%**
- **Hit count increased: 8 → 10**
- Attack range increased
- Launching Strength decreased
- Time gap between shock wave and blood pillar decreased

### Raging Fury Upgrade (TP)
- TP cost at Level 1 decreased: **4 → 2**
- Hit count no longer increased at Level 1

### Thirst
- Overall delay decreased
- Skill Atk. Bonus effect now lasts for its duration
- Strength bonus from certain equipment now applied in real time

### Bloody Twister
- Atk. increased by **19.3%**
- Attack range increased
- Can now be used in the middle of Basic attacks

### Enrage (New Active Skill)
- New Level 35 Active skill
- Strikes ground with weapon, launches enemies, slashes with two weapons, tears into enemies by crossing weapons

### Blood Sword
- Attack range increased
- Blood Sword Explosion effect changed

### Outrage Break
- Atk. increased by **18.6%**
- Character jumps more quickly
- Delay after downward strike decreased
- Downward strike shoots earth shrapnel more quickly
- Can now be used in the middle of Basic attacks

### Blood Memory
- Bleeding enemies will now temporarily increase **Independent Atk., Hit Rate, and Physical Critical Chance**

### Extreme Overkill
- Finishing attack's animation effect renewed
- Moment of striking ground with blood sword: blood explodes, Holding enemies on hit
- Blood shoots up from ground, inflicting multi-hit damage
- Atk. readjusted due to renewal
- Can now be used in the middle of Basic attacks

### Blood Ruin
- **Skill deleted**

### Blood Snatch (New Active Skill)
- New Level 60 Active skill
- Grabs enemy in front, hops and spins with it, slams it onto ground
- Use with Forward key to charge before grab

### Burst Fury
- Atk. increased by **14%**
- Attack range increased
- Time gap between shock wave and blood pillar decreased

### Blood Boom
- Atk. increased by **16.7%**
- Can now be used in the middle of Basic attacks

### Blood Riven
- Atk. increased by **10.1%**
- Can now be used in the middle of Basic attacks

## Bug Fixes
- **Gore Cross**: Additional attacks no longer randomly disabled at high Attack Speed
- **Frenzy**: Atk. bonus by Gore Cross now properly displayed on tooltip

## Chronicle Options Update

### Infinite Onslaught
- 9-piece: Deleted Thirst Atk. +50% vs Bleeding; Added Raging Fury Atk. **+40%**

### Arterial Spray
- 6-piece: Replaced Blood Ruin effects with Blood Snatch Atk. **+30%** and cooldown **-25%**
- 9-piece: Replaced Blood Ruin effects with Blood Snatch Atk. **+40%** and cooldown **-20%**

### Brutal Madman
- 6-piece: Deleted Thirst Atk. +30% vs Bleeding; Added Raging Fury Atk. **+25%**
