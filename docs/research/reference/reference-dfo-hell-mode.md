# Hell Mode (深渊模式) — DFO World Wiki Reference

> Source: https://wiki.dfo.world/view/Hell_Mode
> Scraped: 2026-05-01

---

Hell Mode (aka Hell Parties) is a special dungeon mode that spawns extraordinarily hard enemies with a chance for the strongest equipment.

## Activation Methods

1. **Party Play**: Running dungeons in a party (2+ members) triggers Hell Party after a set number of runs. Larger party = fewer runs needed. Disbanding resets progress. Progress shown via intensity indicator (High = next run triggers).

2. **Forced**: Use Demon Invitations after completing featured quests from Grandis. Works solo or in party.

## Hell Party Flow

1. Room marked with purple demon icon.
2. Pillar appears in center after clearing monsters.
3. Hit pillar → spawn Fallen Guardians.
4. Hit pillar again → spawn one of: Blaze, Miasma, Nightshade, or Darksteel.
5. Then spawn APC group → drops rewards after defeat.

## Variations

### Temporal Rift
- No Epic Souls or Hell Orbs, but higher Epic drop rate.
- Consumes 6 FP on entry, gives 11 Epic Fragments per clear.
- Low chance of special dungeon (Cosmo Fiends, +2 Epic Fragments).
- Epic Wish: select up to 5 Epic Equipment for visual effect on drop (no drop rate change).

### Sky Rift (Harlem)
- 30 Demon Invitations + 2 Rift Sensor Stones per run.
- Chance for Lv 95 Tayberrs equipment.

### Celestial Rift
- 40 Demon Invitations + 4 Rift Sensor Stones, 8 FP.
- Streamlined version, 4 rooms.
- 3 Fiend War bosses randomly appear.
- Gravity Vice drops Antimatter Particles or Special Antimatter Particles.

## Rewards

- Epic Fragments (variable amount)
- Epic Equipment (rare, level depends on region)
- Epic Souls (rare)
- Demon Invitations
- Hell Orbs (region-specific)
- Higher difficulty = higher drop rates for non-Epic rewards
