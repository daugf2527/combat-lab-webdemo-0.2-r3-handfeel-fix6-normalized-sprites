# Guild (公会) — DFO World Wiki Reference

> Source: https://wiki.dfo.world/view/Guild
> Scraped: 2026-05-01

---

Guilds are player-created organizations. Benefits: social connection, special dungeons, rare items, buffs.

## Guild Creation

- Speak to Bucken in Underfoot.
- Requirements: available guild name, 300,000 Gold fee, another player must vouch.
- Default capacity: 300 members (expandable to 1000).

## Ranking (5 Tiers)

| Rank | Icon | Permissions |
|------|------|------------|
| Guild Master | Crown | Full control, disband, transfer role |
| Vice Guild Master | Gold Star | Secondary management |
| Commander | Silver Diamond | Management |
| Sr. Member | Double Chevron | Access public Guild Storage, invite players |
| Jr. Member | Single Chevron | Guild Chat only (default) |

## Alliance

- Partner with one other guild.
- Send buff gifts (stat boosters, EXP boosters) once per day.
- 1-day waiting period after disbanding alliance.

## Guild Hideout

- Unique area with services: Guild Storage, Guild Reinforcer/Refiner (like Kiri/Jun), Exorcism Buffer (+10 Exorcism for 12 runs), Otherverse Portal, Guild Dungeons.

- Reset at end of month.

## Membership

- Join via Guild Menu (';' key), apply to guild.
- Leaving restricts applications for 3 days.
- Guild Contribution Certificates removed from inventory on leave.
