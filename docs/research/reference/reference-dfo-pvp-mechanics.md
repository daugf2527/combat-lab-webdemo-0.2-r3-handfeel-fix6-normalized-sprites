# PvP Mechanics (决斗场机制) — DFO World Wiki Reference

> Source: https://wiki.dfo.world/view/PvP_Mechanics
> Scraped: 2026-05-01

---

PvP in DFO has inner mechanics that mimic both fighting games and beat-em-ups.

## The Axis Planes

- **X axis**: East/West distance.
- **Y axis**: North/South distance (NOT height).
- **Z axis**: Up/Down (height, jumping).

## Combo System

### Standing Combo
- Hitting player while standing, not in air or on ground.
- Moderate hitstun to prevent walking away.
- Damage limit: **red marker** on HP bar. Past red = they fall.

### Aerial / Juggle Combo
- Launch player upward, keep them in air.
- Damage limit: **blue marker** on HP bar. Past blue = fall faster, recover quicker.
- Aerial limit slightly shorter than standing.

### Ground / OTG Combo
- Hitting player laying on ground. Prevents wakeup.
- Damage limit: **yellow marker** on HP bar. Past yellow = quicker wakeup + short ghost frames.
- Ground limit slightly shorter than aerial.

### Wall Combo
- Wall keeps opponent on same X axis, enabling additional combo opportunities.

## Combo Protection

Prevents infinite juggling. Multiple protection forms exist:

### Neutral
- No damage taken for a period, no markers = neutral.
- Base hit rate: 100% (unless overridden by skills like Quick Parry, Bloody Cross).

### Damage Cap
- Past colored markers, combo protection activates: bonus evasion, hit recovery, ghost frames on ground recovery.

### Gravity & Launch Height
- Armor type determines launch height: Cloth (highest) > Leather > Light > Heavy > Plate (lowest).
- Past damage cap, gravity increases — they fall faster and faster. Eventually launches stop working entirely.

### Bonus Evasion & Hit Recovery
- Hitting a player who can't escape temporarily increases their evasion and hit recovery.
- More likely to "miss" or have reduced hitstun.
- Resets on: being hit yourself, or ~3 seconds without getting hit.
- No markers on HP bar = no bonuses.

## Super Armor

- All classes have some form via skills.
- Rapid-hitting attacks slow down super armored players, extending SA duration.
- SA users extend hitboxes, more susceptible to grabs.

## Recovery from Combos

### Wakeup Recovery
- Getting up normally: short invincibility to move away or counter-attack.

### Quick Rebound
- Crouch, invincible + immobile for max 2 seconds, then short SA frames.
- Holding skill increases invincibility time.
- Strategic choice vs regular wakeup — you're vulnerable after SA ends.

### Class-Specific Recovery Skills
- Male Slayers: Flip Side Counter
- Asura: Deadly Enticer
- Female Slayers: Spinning Blade
- Gunners: Secret Land Runner
- Rangers: Revenger
- Female Mages: Phase Shift
- Thieves: Transformation: Molting
- Kunoichi: Ninpou: Decoy

### Scraping (OTG Lift)
- Skills that bounce/grab can lift grounded opponents back into juggle or standing.
- Grabs with shockwave (Knee Kick, Fling, Trap Strike): if NOT bounced to remove shockwave, opponent can stand instantly if juggled past blue marker.
- Grabs without shockwave but airborne (Smasher, BBQ): similar property.
