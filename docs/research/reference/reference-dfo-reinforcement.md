Reinforcement is one of the many systems in Dungeon Fighter Online that is used for the enhancement of the player's gear. With a modest fee of Gold and Clear Cube Fragments, players can reinforce their equipment and weapons, giving them bonus stats and Adventurer Fame.

## Core Rules

- Reinforcing improves accessories, armor, and weapons with bonus properties.
- Costs Gold and Clear Cube Fragments (or Refined Terraniums through Red Tail Jonathan).
- Costs increase depending on item level and reinforcement level.
- Success rate decreases as levels get higher.
- At higher levels, failure permanently destroys the item (unless a Reinforcement Protection Ticket is held, which resets the item to +0 instead).
- Clear Cube Fragments and Vestiges of Hope are given if an item is destroyed.

## Bonus Per Gear Type

| Gear Type | Bonus |
|-----------|-------|
| Weapons | Physical Attack + Magical Attack |
| Armor | Physical Damage Reduction + Overall Damage Reduction |
| Accessories | Magical Damage Reduction + Overall Damage Reduction |
| Sub Equipment & Magic Stones | STR, INT, VIT, SPI |
| Earrings | Physical Attack, Magical Attack, Independent Attack |

- Bonuses before +10 grow additively; after +10 they grow exponentially.

## Safe Reinforcement

- Introduced in Season 7 as a safeguard.
- Each Safe Reinforce failure accumulates adjustment values by 5-10%.
- Level 100+ Unique-Epic Weapons only up to +12.
- Uses Leiern Cores instead of Clear Cube Fragments.

## Success Rate Boosters

- Titles like Master Crafter, Kiri's Favorite
- Reinforcement Proficiency Formula consumable
- High Powered title gives 5% discount
- Portable Reinforce Machines (free but penalties still apply)
- Reinforcement Tickets (free reinforcement to described level)
