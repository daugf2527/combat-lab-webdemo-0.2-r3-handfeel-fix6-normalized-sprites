> Scraped: 2026-05-01

The Auction Hall is a player-driven auction board where items can be bought, bid, and sold. Accessed via `B` key or gavel icon in town.

## Registering an Item

Two price types (at least one required):
- **Buy Now**: Set a fixed purchase price.
- **Initial Bid**: Starting bid price. Bids must be at least 5% higher than previous bid. Can be left blank to disable bidding.

## Restrictions

- Item must be tradable.
- Max 10 items registered (30 with Arad Auction License from CERA shop).
- CERA items (Pet Eggs, Avatars) cannot be registered without Arad Auction License.
- 10,000 Gold deposit per item.

## Duration & Fees

- Listed for 25 hours regardless of owner online status.
- Successful sale: 5% transaction fee deducted + original 10,000 Gold deposit returned.
- Unsold: Item returned via mailbox with deposit.
- Notification via in-game mailbox on sale.
