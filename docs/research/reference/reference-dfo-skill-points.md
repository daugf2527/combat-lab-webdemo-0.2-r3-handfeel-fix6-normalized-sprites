# Skill Points (SP) — DFO World Wiki Reference

> Source: https://wiki.dfo.world/view/Skill_Points
> Scraped: 2026-05-01

---

Skill Points (SP) are used for learning and increasing the level of skills. Players gain a specific amount of SP each time they level up. Skills cost varying amounts of SP to learn and improve.

## SP Per Level (to Lv 95)

| Level | SP Gained | Total SP |
|-------|----------|----------|
| 1 | - | - |
| 2-10 | 30/level | 30→270 |
| 11-20 | 60/level | 330→870 |
| 21-30 | 90/level | 960→1770 |
| 31-50 | 120/level | 1890→4170 |
| 51-60 | 150/level | 4320→5670 |
| 61-70 | 180/level | 5850→7470 |
| 71-80 | 210/level | 7680→9570 |
| 81-90 | 240/level | 9810→11970 |
| 91-95 | 270/level | 12240→13320 |

## Key Observations

- SP gain increases at level brackets: Lv 2-10 (30), Lv 11-20 (60), Lv 21-30 (90), Lv 31-50 (120), Lv 51-60 (150), Lv 61-70 (180), Lv 71-80 (210), Lv 81-90 (240), Lv 91+ (270)
- Total SP at Lv 95 = 13,320
- Earlier levels (1-10) give 30 SP/level; later levels (91+) give 270 SP/level — 9x more
