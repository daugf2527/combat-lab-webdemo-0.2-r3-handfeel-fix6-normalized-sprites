# PvP (决斗场) — DFO World Wiki Reference

> Source: https://wiki.dfo.world/view/PvP
> Scraped: 2026-05-01

---

The Arena is located in Shonan, accessed via Chobung in Underfoot at level 50+. Mode: Individual (1v1) or Captain (3v3).

## PvP Skill Restrictions

- All active skills above level 45 are disabled.
- TP skills are disabled.
- Most skills have rebalanced PvP-specific stats.

## Game Modes

### Rank Arena (Channels 60-61)
- Matchmaking for 1v1 or 3v3 (Captain mode).
- 20 placement matches required first time to determine RP (Elo-like system).
- Rewards: RP, Battle EXP, Victory Tokens.
- Penalty: losing reduces RP and Battle EXP.

### Practice Arena (Channels 101-102)
- No RP/Battle EXP/Victory Token changes.
- Player-created rooms with room list.
- Quickmatch button places in random open room.
- Practice button for APC training.

## Practice Rooms

- 8 slots (1 creator + 7 open).
- Match types: Individual (FFA), Team (Red vs Blue), Captain.
- Observer mode available.
- Map selection available.

## PvP Gear Rules

- Gear has separate PvP stats (check via Arena Info key).
- Higher level/grade/rarity = better PvP stats. Epic > Legendary > Halidom Legacy > Rare Legacy > Unique > Rare Magic Seal > Chronicle > Uncommon > Common.
- Titles, Creatures, Auras, Avatars: 100% of PvE stats apply.
- Disabled in PvP: enchantments (except weapon element), avatar emblems, set effects, Refinement/Amplification bonuses, Earring stats, Insignia stats.
- Reinforcement bonuses have reduced effect (still apply beyond +10).
- Avatar set bonuses still enabled.

## Minimum PvP Gear Recommendations

**Entry Level:**
- Lv 85 Halidom armor/accessories
- Lv 85 Unique sub equipment + magic stone
- Lv 85 Halidom weapon
- Title: 3%+ all speeds, 1%+ hit rate
- Full advanced clone avatars (attack speed/cast speed/movement speed)
- Skin with physical or magic reduction
- Aura + Pet with bonus stats

**Competitive Level:**
- Lv 85 Legendary armor/accessories/sub/magic stone, all +10
- Lv 85 Legendary weapon (elemental damage/enchant), +10
- Title: 3%+ all speeds, 2%+ hit rate
- Full rare clone avatars
- Aura: 2%+ all speeds and/or bonus hit rate
- Pet: 2%+ all speeds and/or bonus hit rate

## Battle EXP Ranks (ascending)

Neophyte → Bronze (4 ranks) → Silver (4 ranks) → Gold (4 ranks) → Diamond (4 ranks) → Terranite (4 ranks)

## RP Ranks

Percentile-based ranks (top %) with 9 rank tiers.

## PvP Networking

- P2P (Peer-to-Peer) system.
- TCP: 10011-10019
- UDP: 5063
- Port forwarding recommended to reduce lag.
