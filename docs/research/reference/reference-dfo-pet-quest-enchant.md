# Part 1: Pet / Creature System (宠物系统)

## Overview

A Creature is a pet that follows the character, boosts stats, and uses skills in battle. Creatures start at level 1 and reach a maximum level of **50**. They level up by converting the character's consumed fatigue points into EXP. Hunger is consumed at 1 point per minute in dungeons; when hunger reaches 0, the pet stops gaining EXP and cannot be used. Hunger restores in town at 10 points per hour.

## Pet Stats

| Category | Stats |
|----------|-------|
| Basic | STR, INT, VIT, SPR |
| Combat | Phys/Mag Crit Rate, Crit Damage, Hit Rate, Evasion, Hit Recovery, AtkSpeed, CastSpeed, MoveSpeed, All Elemental Resistances |
| Utility | Max HP/MP, HP/MP Recovery, Max Inventory Weight, Town Movement Speed, Exorcism Parameter, Dungeon Gold Drop Rate |
| Skill Bonus | Some event creatures provide +1 to specific character skills (e.g., Overdrive, Frenzy) |

## Pet Skills

### Passive Skills
Automatic effects: picking up gold/items, increasing recovery rates, permanent stat buffs.

### Active Skills (V-key)
- Triggered by V key when **blue gauge** is full
- Effects: direct attacks (elemental grenades, lasers, dashes), status effects (stun, freeze, poison, slow), or character buffs

### Overskills
- Triggered by V key when **secondary red gauge** is filled
- Enhanced versions or unique high-tier effects (e.g., Super Armor for party, party-wide damage buffs)
- When red bar is full, creature cannot use its basic skill

## Pet Equipment (Artifacts)

3 equipment slots:

| Slot | Stats Provided |
|------|---------------|
| **Red Artifact** | Attack Amplification, Elemental Damage, STR/INT/VIT/SPR, Evasion, Elem Resist, Max HP/MP |
| **Blue Artifact** | Phys/Mag/Independent Attack, Elemental Attributes, STR/INT/VIT/SPR, Skill MP reduction, Speeds, Crit Rate |
| **Green Artifact** | STR/INT/VIT/SPR, Skill charging time reduction, Phys/Mag Defense, HP/MP recovery on attack, Auto item pickup |

- Rarities: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Unique
- Artifact options only affect the creature, not the player
- All artifacts provide Adventurer Fame
- Rare/Unique artifacts crafted using Malefic Residue via NPC Courier Bannibou recipes

## Pet Acquisition

| Method | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Eggs** | All creatures originate from eggs. Eggs are tradable; hatched pets are character-bound |
| **Item Shop** | Most normal creatures purchased here |
| **Events/Packages** | Limited-edition from themed packages or event milestones |
| **NPC Oran** | Sells artifacts and provides evolution quests |

## Pet Evolution

- Certain creatures evolve, changing name, appearance, and skill power
- **Automatic**: Evolve at specific levels (e.g., Lv. 20)
- **Quest-based**: Complete evolution quests from NPC Oran at specific levels (e.g., Lv. 15, 32, 35, 36)

# Part 2: Quest System (任务系统)

## Quest Categories

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Epic Quests** | Main Story missions that unlock further quests |
| **Side Quests** | Side story introducing game aspects outside main story |
| **Limited Quests** | Daily missions, once per day |
| **Mission Quests** | Series of daily mini-quests in Mission Book |
| **Hidden Quests** | Unlocked under special circumstances |
| **Job Advancement** | Class changes and Awakenings (1st, 2nd, True, Neo) |
| **Creature Quests** | Allow specific creatures to evolve |
| **Event Quests** | Limited-time during special events |
| **Profession Quests** | Choose one of four professions (requires Lv. 20) |
| **Storybooks** | Lore outside Epic Quest lines, re-readable |
| **Daily Challenge** | 3-6 challenges on Bulletin Board, scales with level |
| **Feat Quests** | Related to achievements |

## Quest Structure

- **Acceptance**: Must meet level requirement. Some have prerequisite quests
- **Chains**: Completing auto-unlocks next stage. Epic Quests progress linearly
- **Completion**: Objectives must be met. In scenario dungeons, must redo if failed
- **Party**: Dungeon follows party leader's Epic Quest. Members on same quest progress normally; others get Share Quests for EXP
- **Abandonment**: Most quests can be abandoned except certain advancement quests mid-dungeon

## Quest Name Color Coding (Reward Scaling)

| Color | Meaning | Reward Modifier |
|-------|---------|-----------------|
| **Orange** | No level restriction | 100% Gold, 100% EXP |
| **Red/Yellow** | 0-6 levels lower than character | 100% Gold, 100% EXP |
| **Green** | 7-11 levels lower | -20% Gold, +20% EXP |
| **Grey** | 11+ levels lower | -40% Gold, +40% EXP |

## Quest Rewards

- EXP (thousands to hundreds of thousands)
- Gold (hundreds to 20,000+)
- Items: Cube fragments, consumables, Magic Sealed equipment, selection boxes
- SP: Skill Books (SP +5, SP +20)
- Class Advancement: unlocks new classes and Awakenings
- Creature Evolution: allows pet evolution
- Profession Access: grants profession licenses

## Level Gating

| Milestone | Unlock |
|-----------|--------|
| Level 15 | Class Advancement quests |
| Level 20 | Profession quests, optimal-level Daily Challenges |
| Varies | Scenario dungeons (no level restriction once quest accepted) |
| Varies | Regular dungeons (must meet level + complete Epic Quests for that dungeon) |

# Part 3: Enchant System (附魔系统)

## Overview

Each equipment piece has one enchant slot. Enchantments are permanent — cannot be removed, only overwritten. Enchanted stats display in green text on equipment UI and add Adventurer Fame.

## Enchant Slots

Applicable to: Weapon, Top, Head/Shoulder, Bottom, Belt, Shoes, Necklace, Ring, Bracelet, Sub Equipment, Magic Stone, Earrings, Title

## Card Rarity Tiers

| Rarity | Enchanter Level Required |
|--------|-------------------------|
| Common | Level 1 (Rookie) |
| Uncommon | Level 4 (Blue Moon) |
| Rare | Level 7 (Mother Nature) |
| Unique | Level 10 (Mystical) |
| Legendary | — |
| Epic | Level 11 (Legendary) |

## Card Acquisition

- Monster drops (dungeons, raids)
- Card Albums purchased with materials (e.g., Shattered Red Dusk Crystal)
- NPC Abello: profession quests, card combining/upgrading
- Card Scrolls required to learn how to use specific cards

## Card Synthesis (Compounding)

Combining two cards creates a new one of equal or higher grade:

| Input | Output | Success Rate |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| 2 Common | Uncommon or Rare | 90% Uncommon, 10% Rare |
| 2 Uncommon | Rare or Unique | 76% Rare, 24% Unique |
| 2 Rare | Unique or Legendary | 97% Unique, 3% Legendary |
| 2 Unique | Legendary | 100% Legendary |

- Requires Card Compounder tools (Rookie/Amateur)
- Costs: 10,000 gold (Common), 20,000 (Uncommon), 50,000 (Rare)

## Bead Production

- Beads are converters for fully upgraded Monster Cards
- Allow any player to enchant gear without needing an Enchanter's immediate service
- Requirements: fully upgraded Monster Card + Spirit Essences (Ethereal/Lucid/Zoetic based on rarity) + original enchanting materials
- Spirit Essences provided by Animator NPCs

## Enchant Inheritance

- Transfer enchanted values from one equipment to another corresponding piece
- Requirements: Level 95-115 equipment, Unique+ rarity, fully repaired, not tradable/sealed
- **Cost**: 25,000 Gold or 5 Malefic Residue per part
- Batch: up to 12 parts in a single batch
- Rules:
  - Equipment must be same piece type
  - Target's existing enchant is overwritten
  - Source equipment's values reset to zero
  - Weapon flexibility: classes sharing weapon types can inherit across compatible weapons
- Initiated via Mystical Grimoire (Seria's Room) or specific NPCs

## Enchanter Profession

- **How to become**: Reach Level 20, talk to NPC Abello, turn in 100 Clear Cube Fragments
- **Materials**: Monster Cards, Mana Solvents, Low/High Grade Elemental Crystals, Cube Fragments
- **Mana Extraction**: Disassemble equipment into Mana Solvents (Rookie → Supercritical extractor tiers, up to Level 75 requirement)
- **Special items**:
  - Pure-Eye Charm Bracelet (Lv.5): +5% crafting success rate
  - Enlightened Mage's Thick Spell Book (Lv.9): High-efficiency disassembly
- Enchanters can offer services to other players through player shops
