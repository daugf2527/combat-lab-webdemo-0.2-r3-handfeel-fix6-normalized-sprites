# Party (组队) — DFO World Wiki Reference

> Source: https://wiki.dfo.world/view/Party
> Scraped: 2026-05-01

---

A party is a small unit of up to 4 players.

## Party System

- Party Leader: creates party, chooses dungeon and difficulty, can transfer leadership.
- Party Members: up to 3.
- All members must be in same waiting zone to enter a dungeon.
- Items of Uncommon+ rarity trigger automatic dice roll among party members.
- Leave via ']' key or party icon.

## Starting a Party

Methods:
1. Party UI ('[' key) → Create → name + max members.
2. Left-click character → "Party Invite".
3. `/invite [CharacterName]`.

## Joining a Party

Methods:
1. Party window → browse → "Join".
2. Left-click character → "Join Party".
3. `/join [CharacterName]` — works if in same map, regardless of who is leader.

## Party Benefits

- Party EXP bonus on dungeon completion.
- Party members can use Life Tokens on dead members (and vice versa).
- Leader can enter dungeons/roads not yet unlocked by members.
- Clearing Master's Road with S+ rank unlocks both Master's and King's Road for all members.
- Leader can take entire party into areas they've unlocked, even if members don't meet level requirement.
